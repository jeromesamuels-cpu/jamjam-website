Food safety & control

A disciplined cold chain — not casual vending.

Jam Jam is built as a food-safety system. Patties are made under provincial certification, moved cold, held cold, and only heated after purchase. Every machine is monitored and can be stopped remotely.

Certified, local production

Made in a provincially certified Ontario plant and delivered by refrigerated vehicle straight from the freezer.

Cold-held, heated to order

Stored refrigerated in-machine and heated to a validated safe internal temperature only after the customer orders.

Live temperature telemetry

Continuous temperature monitoring with automatic remote stop-sell on any refrigeration, power or telemetry fault.

Lot & SKU traceability

Every patty is tracked by lot, SKU, date and machine — supporting clean records and rapid mock-recall response.

Documented SOPs

Cleaning, restocking, deviation response and disposal run on standard operating procedures with QA sign-off.

Fast incident response

Target 2–3 hour response on priority incidents and a 98%+ uptime goal across the fleet.

Ingredient, allergen, nutrition and bilingual labelling are provided on every package. Public-health engagement and electrical field approval are completed before any machine is energized on a host site.

Host FAQ

Questions hosts ask.

What does it cost us?

Nothing upfront. There's no cost to the host to run the pilot — you provide approved space, power and service access. You receive a $750/month base location fee (paid quarterly), plus 2% of net sales once the machine clears performance thresholds.

What power does the machine need?

240V, 3-phase, roughly 5,500W. Every site gets an electrical review and field approval before the machine is energized, so we confirm fit before anything is committed.

Will it smell or make a mess?

No. Patties are sealed in their packaging and only heated at the point of sale. There's no open cooking, grease or standing food. We keep the machine clean on a regular service schedule.

Who handles cleaning, restocking and repairs?

We do — entirely. A trained technician restocks by velocity (up to a few times per day if needed), cleans, inspects and removes any expired product. You never touch the machine.

Who handles refunds and customer issues?

Jam Jam does. Each machine has a QR code for instructions, feedback and refunds, backed by our support team.

What about liability and insurance?

Jam Jam carries insurance and retains food-safety responsibility. We provide certificates of insurance and confirm host requirements before installation.

What's the uptime and response if something fails?

We target 98%+ uptime. Machines alert us automatically and can be stopped remotely; we target a 2–3 hour response on priority incidents.

What if it doesn't work out at our site?

The 90-day pilot is designed to be easy to exit. If the location underperforms, we relocate or remove the machine at our cost. No teardown, no cleanup, no obligation for you.
